About Us

Since 2004, Husky Game Development Enterprise (HGD) has been dedicated to producing experienced game developers. We focus on using professional development tools to give students a leg up in the industry, such as Unity, Blender, and Wwise.

After one semester enrolled at MTU, any student is allowed to join an HGD team to create a game throughout the academic year. Teams contain a mix of skills, so new members can learn game development from the ground up, and experienced members can let their creativity flow. There are nearly no limitations enforced, allowing teams to come up with their own idea for the game and develop it for the platform(s) of their choosing.

In the past we've partnered with different sponsoring companies to design games. We also get involved with other events on campus, such as BonzAI Brawl - an AI programming competition that anyone can partake in. This exciting annual event takes a full year to organize and is put together with the joint effort of HGD and the Women in Computing Sciences organization.
